## Band structure

Engineering roles are defined across seven levels (L1-L7). Each level has a
salary band managed in three quartiles. Offers outside a band require
compensation committee approval.

## Confidentiality

Band figures are confidential and shared only with leadership. This page is
restricted: everyone can see the HANDBOOK space, but only the `leadership`
group can retrieve this page.
