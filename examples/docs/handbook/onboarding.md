## First day

New joiners receive their laptop and accounts on day one. Sign the equipment
form before the device is handed over, and complete the security awareness
module within the first week.

## First month

You are assigned a mentor for the first three months, with a weekly one-to-one.
The probation review happens at the end of the second month.
